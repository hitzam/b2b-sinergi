A Frame Triple Range Bottom Loading Arm

The A frame triple range bottom loading (Model 2475) is the ideal solution for when there is limited space in the horizontal direction. The A frame design allows for the arm to be in the vertical position. It then simply pulls out and down for the filling process to begin at ground level bottom loading. Due to the presence of a spring cylinder and swivel joints, this means that the wherever the arm is situated, the final connection will always remain horizontal and ready for filling. The swivel joint also enables the arm to work in both direction with the possible rotation of 360°. Five swivel joints are found on the a frame triple range bottom loading arm, three for the vertical movement and two for the horizontal movement. Often the arm is connected to the pipe through a ANSI 150 flange. An API coupler or dry disconnect coupling can be included for an easy connection to your road or rail tanker.

Dry Disconnect Couplings

Dry Disconnect Couplings are a part of EWFM’s extensive range of couplers. These ensure a spill free, quick and dry connection & disconnection of hoses and pipelines. These have been used in a variety of industries where spillage needs to be minimised. Generally, the products that are pushed through couplings are petroleum, chemical or gas. Aluminium, brass, stainless steel, hastelloy and PEEK are the main materials that our Couplings are available in, however, if you require a more exotic material, they are available on request. Dependant on what material you go for, they all have different working, test and burst pressures.

G457 Hot Refuelling Nozzle

The Model G457 hot refuelling nozzle has been used by the ministry of defence in the United Kingdom since the 1970’s. At the time, a nozzle that could be used to fill aircraft while the turbines and avionics were running was identified as a necessity. The design of this nozzle allows for a rapid turn around of the aircraft safely. It reduces the risk of injury to the operators and the aircraft. The G457 can be used in all sorts of climates as its temperature range is from -30ºC to +52ºC. However, a special low temperature design is available on request. The material of body is resistant to fuels and vapours ensuring a corrosion free long life and fuel will only be delivered when the lever is depressed against the fuselage to prevent spillage. It has a high level shut off therefore protecting the operator and aircraft from spillage when the tanks are full and a froth dissipater allows maximum fuel flow without the shut off operating prematurely.
